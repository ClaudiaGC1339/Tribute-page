Background

Apex Legends is a first-person shooter, battle royale game made by Respawn Entertainment and published by EA. The initial release date was 4th February 2019. The game was released with the original 8 characters: Bangalore, Wraith, Mirage, Bloodhound, Caustic, Gibraltar, Pathfinder and Lifeline.

    Lore

    Characters

    Bloodhound

    Bloodhound is a Recon Legend. Their passive ability "Tracker" can be used to find enemies. Their tactical ability, "Eye of the Allfather", scans the area and reveals hidden enemies and traps. Their ultimate "Beast of the Hunt" causes Bloodhound to gain speed and reveals enemies as well as shoterning the cooldown of "Eye of the Allfather".

    Lifeline

    Lifline is a Support Legend with healing abilities. Her first tactical ability is "D.O.C Heal Drone" which places an immobile drone that heals nearby teammates over time. Her passive "Combat Revive" launches the D.O.C Drone to revive a teammate instead of the player. Finally, the ultimate ability "Care Package" calls down a care package containing useful support gear such as attachments, shields and health kits.

    Bangalore

    Bangalore is an Offensive Legend. Her tactical ability "Smoke Launcher" launches a heavy smokescreen that makes visibility difficult for both enemies and her own team. Her passive ability "Double Time" gives her a movement speed boost when fired at. The ultimate ability "Rolling Thunder" calls several missiles down from the sky to then stun and explode in the indicated area.

    Gibraltar

    Gibraltar is a Defensive Legend. His tactical ability is "Dome of Protection.". Gibraltar creates a dome shield that can be walked through but not fired through. His passive ability "Gun Shield" is a shield that activates whenever Gibraltar is looking down sights and can take some damage before breaking. The ultimate ability is "Defensive Bombardment" which is a cluster strike that damages and stuns within the targeted location.

    Pathfinder

    Pathfinder is also a Recon Legend, similar to Bloodhound. Pathfinder's tactical ability "Grappling Hook", allows you to reach higher places as well as reposition. His appasive ability "Insider Knowledge" allows you to gain information about the next ring location and also lowers the cooldown of his ultimate each time a beacon is scanned. The ultimate ablity "Zipline Gun" provides a zipline for perfect repositioning and fast travel around the map.

    Wraith

    Wraith is another Offensive Legend. Her useful tactical ability allows Wraith to become invincible for 4 seconds. Her passive ability "Voices from the Void" gives her and her teammates audible warnings about possible enemies aiming at her. Finally, her ultimate "Dimensional Rift" places a portal that can help both herself and her team travel distances safely.
